about us

Welcome to 'GOJHARKHAND TAXI & DRIVING SCHOOL'

Book a Taxi Or learn how to drive - One stop for jharkhand roads. Your Trusted Partner for Safe Journeys & Confident Driving – Based in Ranchi, Jharkhand

At GOJHARKHAND TAXI & DRIVING SCHOOL, we pride ourselves on delivering reliable, affordable, and customer-friendly transport and driver training services across Ranchi and surrounding areas. Whether you're planning a comfortable outstation trip, need a quick ride across town, or looking to become a confident and licensed driver, we're here for you—every step (and drive) of the way.

Driven by a passion for excellence and community care, we aim to make every ride safe, every learner skilled, and every customer satisfied. From pickups and drops to driving classes and licensing support, you’ll always find GOJHARKHAND by your side.
