our services

Explore our wide range of rental services

Outstation Rides

Travel beyond Ranchi with ease and comfort. Book our taxis for intercity or outstation journeys with flexible routes and transparent pricing. Ideal for family trips, business travel, or visiting relatives in nearby cities.

Local Rental

Explore Ranchi hassle-free! Our Local Rental service offers cars on an hourly or full-day basis within the city—perfect for shopping, meetings, or casual outings.

Airport, Railway Station & Bus Stand Transfers

On-time pickups and drops to and from Birsa Munda Airport, Ranchi Railway Station, and Bus Stations. Whether you're arriving or departing, we ensure a stress-free experience.

One-Way Taxi

Looking for a convenient ride from one city to another without the return cost? Our One-Way Taxi service allows you to pay for just one side of the journey, saving money and time.
